6. Dodecahedron Mapping

Worked out approximate procedure to project spherical maps onto dodecahedron polyhedral nets. Essentially, each polyhedral face is a separate oblique orthographic projection centered and clipped to the face. Need a good 2d library; maybe GEOS http://trac.osgeo.org/geos/
