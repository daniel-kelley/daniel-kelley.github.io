6. Speaking of pandemic

While cleaning out my car, I found the receipt from Target for a half gallon of organic milk that I bought on 06 March 2020 at the beginning of the Great San Jose Grocery Store Shortage COVID-19 Pandemic Panic because my mom asked me to pick up some milk for dinner that evening. This is memorable because:

(a) This was the fourth store I tried, after trying Save Mart, Seafood City and Walgreens - all had very long lines and no milk.

(b) It was the second-to-last carton of milk in the store.

(c) It was organic, which I hardly ever buy.

(d) I used self-checkout - for the first time ever.

(e) Bought groceries with a credit card - I usually pay cash.

So many principles violated!
